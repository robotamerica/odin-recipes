Simple Baked Potato Recipe

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Coarse Salt
  • Fresh Ground Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 450°F
  2. Prepare baking sheet with wire rack.
  3. Pierce potatos with fork.
  4. Bake first time for 25 minutes, until the skin feels dry and wrinkly.
  5. Remove from oven and brush potatoes with butter or oil.
  6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  7. Return to oven and bake for another 20 minutes.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
  9. Slice open, fluff, and add toppings of your choice.
